Ackee and Saltfish

Description

Ackee and Saltfish is Jamaica's national dish. It is made with salted codfish and ackee, which is the national fruit of Jamaica.

You can use fresh ackee or canned ackee.

Ingredients

  • codfish
  • ackee
  • tomato
  • onion
  • sweet pepper
  • scallion
  • scotch bonnet pepper
  • thyme
